### Fields

1. **Start Hour / End Hour**\
   Defines the daily working hours during which appointments can be booked.
2. **Interval (Minutes)**\
   Sets the duration of each appointment slot (e.g., 30, 60 minutes).
3. **Slot Count**\
   Determines how many appointment slots are available within the defined schedule.
4. **Display Rota**\
   Option to enable or disable displaying the rota (schedule view) for staff and patients.
