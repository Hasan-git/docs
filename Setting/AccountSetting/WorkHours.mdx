---
title: "Workhours"
description: "The Doctor Work Hours page allows administrators to configure and manage a doctor’s available working timeslots. These timeslots define when the doctor is available for appointments and will be reflected in the scheduling and calendar views."
---

### Features

- **Add Work Hours**: Create and assign working timeslots for a doctor across specific days.
- **Display on Schedule/Calendar**: Configured timeslots automatically appear in the schedule to assist staff in booking appointments efficiently.

### Actions

- **Add (\+)** – Opens a form to define new work hour timeslots.
