description: "The Account Settings section is the central place to configure and manage your clinic’s identity within MedRay. Administrators can update clinic information such as name, logo, and contact details, manage branding preferences, and set default system options that affect all users. This section also links to subscription details for billing and plan management and provides administrative controls to ensure smooth daily operations. Overall, it ensures MedRay is properly tailored to your clinic’s workflow, maintaining consistency across users, locations, and patient records."
