title: "Locations"
description: "The Locations page allows administrators to manage all available healthcare locations such as hospitals, clinics, and medical centers. This ensures that appointments and schedules can be tied to the correct facility."
---

### Features

- **Location List**: Displays all registered locations with the following details:
  - **Name** – The name of the hospital, clinic, or center.
  - **Contact** – Contact person or number for the location.
  - **Type** – Type of facility (Hospital, Private Clinic, Medical Center, etc.).
  - **Address** – Physical address of the location.
- **Actions**:
  - **Edit (✎)** – Modify location details.
  - **Delete (⊖)** – Remove a location from the list.
- **New Location**: Add a new hospital, clinic, or center to the system.
