## How to Add Followup:

1. **Open the Patient Profile:**\
   Select the patient whose follow-up you want to create.
2. **Go to the Conditions Section:**\
   Find the specific condition you want to link the follow-up to.
3. **Click “\+Follow-Up”:**\
   Select the option to add a new follow-up.

## How to Edit Followup?

1. **Go to the Patient Profile**
   - Open the patient list and select the patient whose follow-up you want to edit.
2. **Access the Condition**
   - In the patient profile, navigate to the **Conditions** section.
   - Click on the specific condition linked to the follow-up.
3. **Find the Related Follow-Up**
   - Locate the follow-up entry related to that condition.
4. **Click the Edit Icon**
   - Click the **pencil icon** on the right side of the follow-up entry.

     <img
       src="/images/Screenshot2025-07-11204555.png"
       alt="Screenshot2025 07 11204555 Pn"
       title="Screenshot2025 07 11204555 Pn"
       style={{ width:"81%" }}
     />
5. **Edit the Follow-Up Details**
   - Update any necessary fields, such as date, notes, or assigned provider.
6. **Save Changes**
   - Click **Done**  to confirm your changes.
