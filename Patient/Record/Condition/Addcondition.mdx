## How to add a condition?

- **Open Patient Profile:**\
  From the Patients list, select the patient whose medical conditions you want to update.
- **Go to Conditions Section:**\
  Inside the patient profile, find and open the **Conditions** .
- **Click “New Condition”:**\
  Press the **New Condition** button to open the entry form.
- **Enter Condition Details:**\
  Fill in the condition name, diagnosis date, chief complain, and any additional notes or comments.
- **Save the Condition:**\
  Click **Save** to add the condition to the patient’s medical record.

## How to Edit a Condition?

- **Open the Patient Profile:**\
  Select the patient from the patient list whose condition you want to update.
- **Go to the Conditions Section:**\
  Navigate to the **Conditions** within the patient’s profile.
- **Select the Condition to Edit:**\
  Click on the condition you want to modify to open its details.
- **Update Condition Details:**\
  Click **Edit **at the right side of the condition to edit name, notes, diagnoses, or any other relevant information.
- **Save Changes:**\
  Click **Done** to apply the updates to the patient’s record.
