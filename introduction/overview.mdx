# 🧩 Key Modules

MedRay includes a suite of interconnected modules that cover the entire patient and practice lifecycle:

- **Patient Management** – Register, search, and manage detailed patient profiles and medical histories.
- **Appointment Scheduling** – Flexible calendar system for booking, rescheduling, and managing provider availability.
- **Clinical Documentation** – Capture encounter notes using structured templates, SOAP format, and customizable forms.
- **E-Prescriptions** – Send prescriptions digitally, with medication history, dosage validation, and interaction checks.
- **Billing & Insurance** – Generate invoices, process claims, and manage payments with built-in support for insurance workflows.
- **Lab & Imaging Integration** – Place orders, track results, and attach reports directly to patient records.
- **Reports & Analytics** – Monitor clinical, financial, and operational KPIs through configurable dashboards and reports.
- **User Roles & Permissions** – Fine-grained access controls for doctors, nurses, receptionists, admins, and support staff.

---

## ☁️ Deployment

MedRay is available as a:

- **Cloud-Based Application** – Accessible via any modern web browser, with secure remote access for providers and staff.
- **On-Premise Option** – For organizations requiring local hosting due to policy or infrastructure preferences.

---

### 🔐 Security & Compliance

Data security and patient privacy are at the core of MedRay's architecture:

- ✅ Fully HIPAA-compliant
- ✅ End-to-end encryption (AES-256)
- ✅ Role-based access control (RBAC)
- ✅ System audit logs for traceability
- ✅ Daily secure backups and recovery protocols

---

### 🔄 Integrations & Interoperability

MedRay supports modern healthcare interoperability standards:

- **FHIR** and **HL7** protocols
- **RESTful API** for third-party integrations
- Secure webhooks for real-time event notifications
- Integration-ready for labs, imaging centers, pharmacies, and billing providers

---

### 👥 User Types & Access

MedRay is designed for use across various healthcare roles:

- 👨‍⚕️ **Doctors** – Chart encounters, prescribe medications, review lab results
- 👩‍⚕️ **Nurses** – Triage patients, update vitals, assist in documentation
- 🧑‍💼 **Receptionists** – Manage appointments, patient intake, and front-desk workflows
- 🛠️ **Admins/IT** – Configure users, settings, permissions, and integrations
